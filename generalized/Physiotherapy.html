Physiotherapy

physiotherapy services specialize in rehabilitative care aimed at restoring movement and function to patients with physical impairments or disabilities. The hospital's physiotherapists assess each patient's condition and design personalized treatment plans using therapeutic exercises, manual therapy techniques, and specialized equipment. They cater to various conditions such as musculoskeletal injuries, neurological disorders, post-operative recovery, and chronic pain management. The goal of physiotherapy at Tema General Hospital is to enhance mobility, alleviate pain, improve strength and flexibility, and ultimately promote patients' overall quality of life through targeted rehabilitation programs.
