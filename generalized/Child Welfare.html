Child Welfare

Tema General Hospital's child welfare services encompass a range of healthcare provisions aimed at promoting the well-being and development of children. This typically includes regular pediatric check-ups to monitor growth, development milestones, and overall health. The hospital offers vaccinations according to national schedules to protect children from preventable diseases. Additionally, they provide nutritional counseling and support to ensure proper feeding and dietary needs are met during crucial developmental stages. Child welfare services at Tema General Hospital emphasize preventive care, early detection of health issues, and holistic support for families to foster optimal health outcomes for children in their care.
