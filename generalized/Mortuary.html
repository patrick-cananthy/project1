Mortuary Services

The mortuary services provide respectful and dignified handling of deceased individuals, supporting grieving families during difficult times. These services include the safe storage and preservation of bodies, adhering to strict hygiene and legal standards. The mortuary staff assists families with necessary paperwork and arrangements for viewing and release of the deceased. They ensure confidentiality and compassion in handling each case, offering support to families coping with loss. Tema General Hospital's mortuary services aim to provide a comforting environment and reliable assistance to families as they navigate the process of saying goodbye to their loved ones.
