Corporate Medical

The corporate medical services cater specifically to the health needs of businesses and organizations. These services are designed to promote employee health and well-being, thereby enhancing productivity and reducing absenteeism. The hospital typically offers comprehensive health assessments, including medical check-ups, screenings for chronic diseases, and vaccinations tailored to occupational health requirements. Corporate medical services at Tema General Hospital may also include occupational health consultations, where healthcare professionals provide advice on workplace safety, ergonomic assessments, and management of work-related injuries. They may collaborate with companies to develop health promotion programs, addressing topics such as stress management, nutrition, and fitness. Overall, Tema General Hospital's corporate medical services aim to support businesses in maintaining a healthy workforce, fostering a safe work environment, and promoting overall employee wellness.
