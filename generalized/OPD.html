Out Patient Department

The Tema General Hospital offers comprehensive Outpatient Department (OPD) services catering to a wide range of medical needs. Patients can access various healthcare services without requiring admission, including consultations with specialists across different fields such as internal medicine, pediatrics, obstetrics and gynecology, surgery, dermatology, and more. The OPD also provides diagnostic services such as laboratory tests, imaging (X-rays, ultrasound), and outpatient procedures. With a focus on patient care and efficient service delivery, the Tema General Hospital OPD aims to provide timely and effective medical attention to its community.

Availability:

  • Mondays - Sundays: 24/7
