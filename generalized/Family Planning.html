Family Planning

family planning services designed to meet the reproductive health needs of individuals and couples. These services typically include counseling on various contraceptive methods tailored to personal preferences and health considerations. The hospital provides access to a range of contraceptives such as birth control pills, condoms, intrauterine devices (IUDs), injectables, and implants. Additionally, they offer guidance on fertility awareness methods and provide support for preconception care. The focus is on promoting informed decision-making and ensuring safe and effective family planning practices for the community served by the hospital.
