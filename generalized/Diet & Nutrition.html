Diet & Nutrition

diet and nutrition services focus on promoting health and well-being through personalized dietary guidance and education. The hospital's nutritionists and dietitians provide expert advice tailored to individual health needs, addressing concerns such as weight management, chronic disease prevention (e.g., diabetes, hypertension), and specialized dietary requirements. They offer consultations where patients receive personalized meal plans, nutritional assessments, and practical tips for adopting healthy eating habits. The services emphasize the importance of balanced nutrition in supporting overall health and recovery from illness. Tema General Hospital's diet and nutrition services aim to empower patients to make informed choices that optimize their nutritional intake and improve their quality of life.
