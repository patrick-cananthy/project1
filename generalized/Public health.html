Public Health

Tema General Hospital's public health services are dedicated to promoting and protecting the health of the community through various preventive and health education initiatives. These services typically include vaccination programs, disease surveillance, and outbreak management to prevent the spread of communicable diseases. The hospital also conducts health screenings, such as for hypertension and diabetes, to detect and manage chronic conditions early. Additionally, they offer health education and awareness campaigns on topics like nutrition, sanitation, family planning, and hygiene practices. The focus of Tema General Hospital's public health services is on empowering individuals and communities to adopt healthy behaviors and lifestyles, thereby improving overall community health outcomes.
