Herbal CLinic

The herbal clinic services at Tema General Hospital offer alternative and complementary medicine options alongside conventional treatments. These services typically involve consultations with trained herbalists who provide personalized herbal remedies tailored to individual health needs. The clinic focuses on using natural herbs and traditional healing practices to address various health conditions, aiming to complement mainstream medical care. Patients receive guidance on herbal treatments, their benefits, potential side effects, and how they can integrate with conventional treatments. The herbal clinic at Tema General Hospital aims to provide holistic healthcare options that respect cultural practices and promote patient choice in managing their health.
