Clinical Psychology

clinical psychology services provide essential mental health care through evidence-based interventions and therapeutic support. The hospital's clinical psychologists specialize in assessing and treating a variety of psychological and emotional issues, including anxiety disorders, depression, trauma-related conditions, and adjustment difficulties. They offer individual counseling sessions where patients receive personalized treatment plans tailored to their specific needs and goals. The services may also include psychoeducation, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), and other therapeutic modalities aimed at enhancing coping skills, improving emotional well-being, and fostering resilience. Tema General Hospital's clinical psychology services strive to promote mental health awareness and support patients in achieving better psychological health and overall quality of life.
