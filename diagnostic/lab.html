Laboratory Services

The laboratory services play a vital role in diagnostic medicine by providing accurate and timely testing for a wide range of medical conditions. Staffed by trained professionals, the hospital's laboratories conduct various tests including blood tests, urine analysis, microbiological cultures, and specialized investigations such as genetic testing or tumor markers. These services support healthcare providers in diagnosing diseases, monitoring treatment effectiveness, and detecting health issues early. Our General Hospital's commitment to quality assurance and adherence to international standards ensures reliable results, contributing to optimal patient care and management.
