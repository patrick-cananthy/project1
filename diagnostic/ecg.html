Electrocardiogram (E.C.G) Services

Our General Hospital's Electrocardiogram (E.C.G) services provide crucial diagnostic tools for assessing heart health. ECGs record the electrical activity of the heart, producing a visual representation (electrocardiogram) that helps healthcare providers identify irregularities in heart rhythm, detect signs of heart disease, and monitor cardiac function. This non-invasive procedure involves placing electrodes on the chest, arms, and legs to capture the heart's electrical signals. ECG services at Our General Hospital are essential for diagnosing conditions like arrhythmias, heart attacks, and evaluating overall cardiac health. They play a pivotal role in guiding treatment decisions and ensuring timely interventions to manage cardiovascular conditions effectively.
