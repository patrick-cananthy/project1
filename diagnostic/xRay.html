X-RAY

Our X-ray diagnostic services offer essential imaging capabilities to aid in the diagnosis and management of various medical conditions. The hospital's radiology department utilizes advanced X-ray technology to capture detailed images of bones, organs, and tissues. These images assist healthcare providers in diagnosing fractures, detecting abnormalities, and monitoring treatment progress. The X-ray services at Our General Hospital prioritize patient safety and accuracy, ensuring that imaging procedures are conducted with precision and adherence to radiation safety protocols. The department plays a critical role in supporting healthcare teams by providing timely and reliable diagnostic information to guide effective patient care and treatment decisions.
