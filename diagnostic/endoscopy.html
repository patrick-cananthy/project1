Endoscopic

Our endoscopic services offer advanced diagnostic and therapeutic procedures using minimally invasive techniques. Endoscopy involves inserting a flexible tube with a light and camera into the body's natural openings or through small incisions to visualize internal organs and structures. This allows healthcare providers to examine the digestive tract (including the esophagus, stomach, and intestines), respiratory system, and other internal organs. Endoscopic procedures can help diagnose conditions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, polyps, and cancers. Additionally, therapeutic endoscopy may involve interventions like removing polyps, treating bleeding, or placing stents to relieve obstructions. Our General Hospital's endoscopic services prioritize patient comfort and safety, providing essential diagnostic insights and treatments while minimizing recovery time and discomfort compared to traditional surgical approaches.
