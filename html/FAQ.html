What are the visiting hours?

Visiting hours are from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Sunday. Please note that visiting hours may vary by department.

How can I make an appointment?

You can make an appointment by calling our main line at (0303) 123-456 or by using our online appointment system on our website.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans including National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) and private health insurance providers. Please contact our billing department for specific inquiries.

Where is the hospital located?

Tema General Hospital is located at Hospital Road, Community 11, Tema. You can find directions on our website or contact us for more details.

How do I obtain my medical records?

To obtain your medical records, please visit the Medical Records department with a valid ID and fill out the necessary forms. You can also request your records via email at records@temageneralhospital.com.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services. Please go directly to our Emergency Department located at the front of the hospital for immediate assistance.

What specialties are available at Tema General Hospital?

We offer a wide range of specialties including Cardiology, Neurology, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, and more. Please visit our specialties page on our website for a complete list.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team via email at support@temageneralhospital.com or call us at (0303) 123-456. Our team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
