Hypertension

Tema General Hospital's hypertension services are dedicated to managing high blood pressure, a prevalent condition that requires ongoing care to reduce the risk of cardiovascular complications. Patients receive comprehensive diagnosis through blood pressure monitoring and related assessments. Treatment plans are tailored to individual needs, emphasizing lifestyle modifications such as diet and exercise, alongside medications when necessary. Regular monitoring and check-ups help track blood pressure levels and adjust treatment as needed to maintain optimal health. Education on hypertension management and potential complications is provided to empower patients in their journey towards better cardiovascular health. Tema General Hospital's hypertension services aim to prevent complications and improve the overall well-being of patients through effective management and support.
