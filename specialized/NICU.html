NICU

The Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) is dedicated to providing specialized care for newborn infants who require intensive medical attention. Staffed by neonatologists, nurses trained in neonatal care, and other healthcare professionals, the NICU offers a high level of monitoring and treatment for premature babies, critically ill newborns, and those born with complications. Services include respiratory support, intravenous therapies, temperature regulation, feeding assistance, and management of medical conditions unique to newborns. The NICU at Tema General Hospital plays a crucial role in providing compassionate and expert care to ensure the best possible outcomes for newborns during their critical early days of life.
