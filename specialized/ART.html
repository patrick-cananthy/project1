ART Clinic

The ART (Antiretroviral Therapy) clinic at Tema General Hospital specializes in the management and treatment of HIV/AIDS. Led by infectious disease specialists and trained healthcare professionals, the clinic provides comprehensive care to individuals living with HIV. Services offered include HIV testing, initiation of antiretroviral therapy (ART), monitoring of viral load and CD4 count, management of HIV-related complications, and provision of counseling and support services. The clinic focuses on ensuring adherence to treatment protocols, promoting healthy living practices, and empowering patients with knowledge about HIV prevention and management. With a patient-centered approach, the ART clinic at Tema General Hospital aims to enhance the health outcomes and quality of life for individuals affected by HIV/AIDS in the community.
