Dermaetology Clinic

The dermatology clinic at Tema General Hospital specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail conditions. It is staffed by dermatologists who are skilled in managing a wide range of dermatological issues, including acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin infections, allergies, and skin cancers. The clinic offers comprehensive services such as consultations, diagnostic procedures (like skin biopsies and patch testing), and various treatment options including topical therapies, systemic medications, and minor surgical procedures. Patient education is emphasized to promote skin health and prevention of skin disorders. The dermatology clinic at Tema General Hospital aims to provide personalized care, improve skin conditions, and enhance the overall well-being of patients through effective management and ongoing support.
