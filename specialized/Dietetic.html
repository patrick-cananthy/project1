Dietetic

dietetic services focus on providing specialized nutritional support and guidance to patients based on their medical conditions and individual needs. Patients receive personalized dietary assessments and counseling sessions aimed at promoting health, managing chronic conditions, and supporting recovery. The hospital's dietitians work closely with healthcare teams to develop tailored meal plans that consider patients' medical histories, preferences, and cultural backgrounds. They educate patients on healthy eating habits, portion control, and the importance of nutrition in overall health. Tema General Hospital's dietetic services aim to empower patients to make informed food choices and adopt sustainable dietary practices that enhance their well-being and support their medical treatment plans.
