Chest Clinic

Tema General Hospital's chest clinic specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of respiratory and chest-related conditions. Patients visiting the chest clinic typically receive comprehensive evaluations for respiratory symptoms such as coughing, shortness of breath, chest pain, and lung infections. The clinic offers a range of services including diagnostic tests such as spirometry (lung function tests), chest X-rays, and CT scans to accurately diagnose conditions like asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), pneumonia, and tuberculosis (TB). Treatment plans are tailored to each patient's specific needs and may include medications, inhalation therapy, lifestyle recommendations, and pulmonary rehabilitation programs to improve lung function and overall respiratory health. The chest clinic at Tema General Hospital is committed to providing specialized care, education, and support to help patients manage respiratory conditions effectively and enhance their quality of life.
