Anaesthesia

The Anesthesia Department is essential in providing safe and effective anesthesia care for surgical procedures, pain management, and critical care situations. Led by anesthesiologists, the department ensures patient comfort and safety before, during, and after surgical procedures by administering appropriate anesthesia medications tailored to individual needs. They also monitor vital signs closely throughout procedures to maintain optimal physiological conditions. Beyond surgery, the department may also provide anesthesia services for obstetric deliveries, emergency interventions, and diagnostic procedures. The Anesthesia Department at Tema General Hospital plays a critical role in supporting various medical specialties by ensuring pain relief and maintaining patient stability during medical procedures.
