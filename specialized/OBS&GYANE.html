OBS & GYNAE

The Obstetrics and Gynecology (OBS & GYNAE) department specializes in women's reproductive health, offering comprehensive care from adolescence through menopause and beyond. Staffed by obstetricians, gynecologists, midwives, and specialized nurses, the department provides a wide range of services including prenatal care, childbirth, postnatal care, family planning, management of gynecological issues such as menstrual disorders, infertility, and gynecological cancers. The department also offers diagnostic services like ultrasound imaging and screenings, along with both medical and surgical treatments tailored to women's specific health needs. Emphasizing patient education, the OBS & GYNAE department at Tema General Hospital strives to empower women with knowledge about their health, ensuring they receive personalized care and support throughout their reproductive and gynecological journey.
